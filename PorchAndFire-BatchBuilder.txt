BRAND VOICE & STYLE RULES
PorchAndFire targets homeowners ages 40-60 who are actively investing in their outdoor spaces. Think: fire pits, patio furniture, pergolas, privacy landscaping, and backyard entertaining. The brand identity is warm, earthy, and aspirational but grounded.
Writing Rules:
	•	Write like a knowledgeable neighbor who has great taste and actually uses this stuff. Not a salesperson, not a lifestyle blogger.
	•	No em dashes. No semicolons. Use periods and commas.
	•	Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max).
	•	Conversational but not sloppy. No excessive exclamation points.
	•	Address the reader as 'you' naturally.
	•	Every post should feel like it saves the reader time, money, or a bad purchase decision.
	•	Do NOT use filler phrases like 'In today's fast-paced world' or 'Let's dive in' or 'Without further ado'.
	•	Lead with the value. First sentence should hook or promise a specific outcome.
	•	Mention real-world use cases: 'great for entertaining 6-8 people', 'works on a 10x10 patio', 'holds up through NC winters'.
	•	Our take / verdict sections should feel personal and opinionated, not generic.

AFFILIATE LINK FORMAT
All Amazon affiliate links use this format:
https://www.amazon.com/s?k=[URL+encoded+product+search+query]&tag=porchandfire-20
The tag is always porchandfire-20. Use Amazon search URLs (not direct product ASINs) so links stay valid even if specific products go out of stock.
Example: A search for 'solo stove bonfire' becomes: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=solo+stove+bonfire&tag=porchandfire-20
